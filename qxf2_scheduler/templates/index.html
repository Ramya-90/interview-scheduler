We have developed this application to handle large volume of candidates during our interviews. You can refer the source code here.

  1. Allow us to handle a large volume of candidates without having to bother about scheduling.
  2. Make the interaction with Qxf2 different from other companies
  3. Remind candidates at key moments during the process about Qxf2's features
  4. Reduce (almost eliminate) exchange of emails between candidate and interviewer
Interview scheduler has three main entities. They are Jobs, Interviewers and Candidates.

To add yourself as an interviewer

  1. Go to /interviewers
  2. Add your details link name, email id and your available time slots
  3. You can give any number of time slots with breaks

To add candidates

  1. Add the candidates details
  2. Add some comments about the candidates if it's specified in their resume or in the email

To add job

  1. Add the Job with interviewers names
  2. Add the rounds for the job also

General instructions

  1. Once the interview is scheduled we can reschedule it through our application. We can further do it through Email. Interviewer should take care of it
  2. Add your feedback once the interview is over. It will be helpful for the next round of the interviewer.
  3. If you are the last round of the interviewer don't send any reject/accept to the candidates. We will take care of it.
